Film / Graphics / Web

What else is there to know?
...you may ask. Well i'm a 21-year old creative soul from the town of Huskvarna, Sweden. From an early age creativity has captivated me. Beginning in the world of filmmaking my journey started with making slapstick short films with my friends and selling Minecraft intros on YouTube.

Over the years, I've honed my skills in Film, Graphic Design, and Web. I'm well-versed in Adobe After Effects, Premiere Pro, Photoshop, and Illustrator, allowing me to bring my visual ideas to life with ease. In the world of web development, I'm fluent in the languages of JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, which means I can not only design but also bring my creations to life on the web, with the help of Figma of course. One example is this portfolio, entirely coded and created by me!

I'm so glad you're here to visit my portfolio, wether you're reviewing me for work or just seeking some creative inspiration, you're very welcome! I'm eager to share my passion with you and hope you won't hesitate to contact me if you need help, have any questions or just feel like connecting!

"Oh, by the way... I think the TV could use a kick!"
